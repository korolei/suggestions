Electronic Suggestion Box

Your feedback is important to us!
All staff are encouraged to submit comments or suggestions on how to improve the OFA. Entries are submitted anonymously.
The Electronic Suggestion Box is monitored on a daily basis. Every effort will be made to post a response to new entries within 3 business days.
Consistent with the government's workplace discrimination and harassment prevention directive, the OFA will not accept any comments/suggestions that contain inappropriate language or statements.
